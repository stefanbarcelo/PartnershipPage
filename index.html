Our industry standard inspection process

Exceeding all expectations

As a True360 Dealer, you will be provided with the dedicated support staff which includes an account executive as well as a group of in market Certified Automotive Inspectors who are dispatched on demand as needed.

Preliminary evaluation and paint metering

First we do a thorough and hands on preliminary vehicle walk around evaluation of the overall condition. After, we do a complete metering of all paint surfaces and finish, both outside and inside the vehicle structure.

Structural Inspection

We inspect the complete uni-body, structure, or frame of the vehicle to be sure it is within the manufacturers specifications. This includes the vehicles center rails, floor pan, inner rockers, and cross member.

The True360 Report

Our detailed report is uploaded to both CARFAX and AutoCheck daily. It's an easy to read four page document that comes complete with the vehicle's certificate of integrity. Each report is automatically delivered to you.
